About

Fake news a.k.a Hoax news occupies a large sphere of cyberspace today worldwide. States, Institutions as well as Individuals are using fake news as a tool of publicity.

We developed this Web App for verifying news sources from multiple news sites and tagging their articles as real or fake. The app also provides services for checking whether a news article is fake and finding fake news spreading bot. Now, all of us can read and rely on the real news.

Services

VERIFY NEWS provides multiple news related services - a daily news feed of verified content curated from multiple news sources, an app that utilises the power of Machine Learning (ML) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to detect fake news and another app to find fake news spreading bots.
