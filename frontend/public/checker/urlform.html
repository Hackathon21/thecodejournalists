How do we do it?

We trained a ML model by providing it with thousands of examples of real and fake news. Th Model analyzes the text content of the news articles and tries to make sense of its autheticity and returns a probabilistic result.

Why is it a bit slow?

Like we said, we trained the models by using thousands of examples. Hence the size of ML model is too big. We are also creating word clouds and pie chart along with fetching related news in the background.

How accurate it is?

The ML model have been tested to give correct results 9/10 times. The accuracy score of the trained model remains between 0.68 to 0.80 for different models. Since we also provide related news articles, it's easy to identify the real news.
